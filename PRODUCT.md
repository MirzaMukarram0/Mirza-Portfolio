# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Tech recruiters, startup founders, hiring managers for full-stack web developer/AI software engineer roles specializing in web-based AI solutions.

## Product Purpose
To secure a full-time role, get freelance clients, stand out as a rigorous engineer, and explicitly highlight design and frontend engineering skills.

## Brand Personality
Rigorous, classic, editorial.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS templates, modern neon tech themes. Should strictly maintain a classic, creative editorial/newspaper theme.

## Design Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell:** Demonstrate frontend engineering excellence through the interface itself.
- **Classic Creativity:** Use the newspaper aesthetic to stand out from typical tech portfolios without compromising professionalism.
- **Rigorous Execution:** Maintain high technical standards, performance, and accessibility.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard best practices, adhering to WCAG guidelines for contrast and reduced motion.
